What is Lavender?

Lavender is well-known for its calming aroma and therapeutic properties. Used in traditional and modern medicine, lavender helps reduce anxiety, improve sleep, and relieve pain. It is also commonly used in skincare, teas, and essential oils for its soothing effects.

Benefits of Lavender in Traditional and Modern Medicine

Benefit Traditional Use Modern Medicine
Relaxation Promote relaxation and reduce stress. Shown to reduce anxiety and improve mood.
Sleep Aid Used to enhance sleep quality. Improves sleep by reducing restlessness.
Pain Relief Applied to relieve headaches and muscle pain. Effective in reducing migraines and body pain.
Skin Care Soothe skin irritation and promote healing. Used in skincare for its anti-inflammatory properties.
