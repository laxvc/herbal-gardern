What is Dandelion?

Dandelion is known for its nutritional benefits and is used in both traditional and modern medicine. Its roots, leaves, and flowers are consumed for their anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and diuretic properties. Dandelion is often used in teas and supplements to improve digestion and liver health.
