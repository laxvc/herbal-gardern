What is Tulsi?

Tulsi, also known as Holy Basil, is revered in traditional medicine for its remarkable therapeutic properties. Known for its adaptogenic and anti-inflammatory benefits, Tulsi plays a significant role in promoting overall wellness and spiritual health.

Benefits of Tulsi in Traditional and Modern Medicine

Benefit Traditional Use Modern Medicine
Adaptogen Used to enhance the body's response to stress. Modern studies confirm its role in reducing stress and improving mental clarity.
Anti-inflammatory Applied to reduce inflammation and support joint health. Used in various supplements for its anti-inflammatory properties, aiding in conditions like arthritis.
Immune Support Traditionally used to boost immunity and fight infections. Contains compounds that enhance the immune system and offer protection against various pathogens.
Respiratory Health Used to treat respiratory issues like coughs and colds. Often included in remedies for respiratory health due to its expectorant and antimicrobial properties.
Digestive Health Consumed to aid digestion and alleviate gastrointestinal discomfort. Helps in managing digestive issues and promotes overall gut health.
