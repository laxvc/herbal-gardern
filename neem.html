What is Neem?

Neem is known for its potent medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its leaves, bark, and seeds are used for their antibacterial, antiviral, and antifungal properties. Neem is commonly used in skincare and as a natural remedy for various ailments.

Benefits of Neem in Traditional and Modern Medicine

Benefit Traditional Use Modern Medicine
Antibacterial Used to treat infections and promote skin health. Modern studies confirm its efficacy in treating bacterial infections and improving skin conditions.
Antiviral Applied to manage viral infections and boost immune function. Has been shown to have antiviral properties, supporting its use in preventing and treating viral infections.
Antifungal Used to combat fungal infections and support overall health. Effective in treating fungal infections, including those of the skin and nails.
Detoxification Traditionally used to detoxify the body and improve liver function. Supports liver health and detoxification processes, aiding in overall wellness.
Skin Care Applied topically to treat acne and other skin conditions. Commonly included in skincare products for its beneficial effects on skin health.
