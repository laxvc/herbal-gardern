What is Aloe Vera?

Aloe Vera is a succulent plant known for its medicinal properties. It is commonly used in skincare, wound healing, and digestive aid. Aloe Vera gel, extracted from the plant's leaves, contains anti-inflammatory and soothing compounds.

Benefits of Aloe Vera in Traditional and Modern Medicine

Benefit Traditional Use Modern Medicine
Wound Healing Applied to burns and wounds for faster healing. Used in modern skincare to treat burns and cuts.
Digestive Aid Consumed to relieve constipation and improve digestion. Aloe Vera is known for its soothing effects on digestion.
Skin Care Used to soothe skin irritations and rashes. Common ingredient in skincare for hydrating and soothing skin.
Anti-inflammatory Used traditionally to reduce inflammation. Modern medicine uses it for its anti-inflammatory properties.
