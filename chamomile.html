What is Chamomile?

Chamomile (Matricaria chamomilla) is a well-known herb used for its calming properties. It's widely used in teas to promote relaxation and sleep, as well as in skin care for its soothing effects.

Benefits of Chamomile in Traditional and Modern Medicine

Benefit Traditional Use Modern Medicine
Relaxation Used in teas to reduce stress and anxiety. Promotes relaxation, helps reduce symptoms of anxiety and aids sleep.
Digestive Health Soothes upset stomachs and relieves indigestion. Commonly used to alleviate digestive issues such as bloating and gas.
Anti-inflammatory Applied to the skin to reduce irritation and inflammation. Contains anti-inflammatory compounds that support skin health.
Sleep Aid Promotes restful sleep when consumed before bed. Included in many sleep remedies to help improve sleep quality.
